About Me

I am Winsandar Phyo. I graduated with a B.A. in English from Hinthada University (2012-2016) and officially received my degree in 2017. Later that year, from June to October 2017, I attended the PPTT Teacher Training Program (Batch 4/17) at Pathein Education Degree College.
In June 2018, I began my professional career as an Assistant Primary School Teacher under the Ministry of Education, where I served until June 2021. During my free time between jobs, I taught students as a tuition teacher. After that, in October 2023, I joined the Department of Social Welfare as a Junior Clerk, where I worked until February 2025.
Due to family responsibilities, including caring for my mother during her eyes surgery, I decided to resign from my second job.
Currently, I am attending a Web Development course at HeinLearn to build the necessary skills for my future career. My goal is to become a work-from-home professional freelancer and web developer, combining my background in education with new technical expertise.
